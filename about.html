Rooted in Dreamland Beach culture since day one.

We built Bali’s most trusted surf school on three non-negotiables: safety, local knowledge, and unforgettable ocean fun.

Our story

From beach shack to full-spectrum coaching.

Dreamland Surf School was founded by local watermen Erik and Pechey to protect the integrity of lessons delivered on their home break. We are still 100% local-owned and operate exclusively from our beach station on Dreamland Beach, Pecatu.

Today, travellers and Bali residents trust us for their first pop-up, progression weeks, or simply renting the right board for the day. We keep our footprints light, our smiles big, and our standards professional.

Values we live by

  • Safety before everything: accredited coaches + structured risk briefings.
  • Respect for the ocean & local community.
  • Transparent pricing and direct communication channels only.
  • Fun-first culture—progression sticks when you enjoy the ride.

Safety & accreditation

Bronze Medallion & ISA standards.

All instructors are certified in Bronze level Lifesaving Surf Rescue, first aid, and CPR. Regular mock drills ensure we respond within seconds on crowded days. You’ll warm up on the beach, review rip-current escape plans, and know exactly whom to call if you need help.

We match every student with the right zone (inside foamies, mid-bank, or reef shoulder) depending on tides and confidence. Parents can watch comfortably from our shaded base while kids learn using soft-top boards and fun safety games.
