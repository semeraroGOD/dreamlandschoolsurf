Home / Lessons

Lesson formats for every surfer and every tide.

Pick the coaching structure that fits your goals. Each session includes spot brief, equipment, and post-surf feedback.

1:1 Focus

Private Lesson

90 minutes tailored to your pace. Ideal for first pop-up, fine-tuning turns, or confidence refresh.

  • Personal coach + dedicated photographer add-on.
  • Video breakdown or on-beach feedback session.
  • Board changes available mid-lesson.

Pairs

Semi-Private

Two surfers of similar level share a coach. Perfect for couples, siblings, or best friends.

  • Skill assessment to sync both surfers.
  • Alternate heat-style coaching for faster rest & reset.

Families & squads

Group Sessions

Up to five surfers with an instructor + beach marshal for safety. Fun warm-ups and micro-challenges.

  • Custom ratio for kids under 12.
  • Shared recap + digital certificate for the crew.

Little groms

Kids Club

Foam boards, shallow sections, and ocean games to keep confidence high and wipeouts soft.

  • Parents welcome to stay in the water.
  • Includes safety briefing made fun with props.

Board rentals

Soft-top & fibreglass quiver.

We stock everything from 6'0 performance shortboards to 9'0 soft logs. Every rental comes with a signed agreement handled directly by Dreamland staff—third parties using our name are not covered.

  • Swap boards mid-session if the tide changes.
  • Rash guards & reef booties available.
  • Storage + sweet water rinse at the beach station.

Lesson flow

  1. Meet Erik or Pechey at the Dreamland Surf School flag.
  2. Quick briefing on tide, crowd, and safety zones.
  3. Warm-up + land drills tailored to today’s objective.
  4. In-water reps with real-time tips.
  5. Cool down, review wins, plan the next session.
